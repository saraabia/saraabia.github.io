01, About

I'm a designer who came up through data. After a BSc in Data Science at the University of Warwick, I'm now pursuing an MSc in Digital Social Media at USC, chasing the part of design that lives where research, story, and product converge.

I co-founded a boutique hotel during the pandemic and ran every part of the guest experience: website, brand, booking flow, in-room collateral, the whole thing. I won "Best Design" at Goldman Sachs for a UX challenge, shipped a consumer site through Kode with Klossy, and now lead strategy & analytics on a live consumer-brand campaign at USC.

I think the best design comes from understanding people deeply, then sweating the details. I'm trilingual (English, French, Arabic), an active builder with AI tooling, and genuinely happiest when I'm jumping between a Figma file and a customer interview.

02, Selected Work

Four projects I'm proud of, and what each one taught me about shipping for real people.

CASE 01 / 04

A boutique hotel, rebuilt around the guest

Brand · Web · UX 2020 to 2024 Solo + Co-founder

When COVID-19 collapsed our family riad's bookings, I redesigned the entire guest experience, website, brand, booking flow, in-room collateral, using guest reviews as my primary research tool. Reservations climbed 30% and revenue 40%, while our rating reached 4.8/5.

CASE 02 / 04

An expense tracker that won "Best Design"

Product · UX · Prototype Goldman Sachs · 2022 Lead Designer

During Goldman Sachs' Technical Product Development challenge, I led the UX design of a consumer-facing personal expense tracker. We won "Best Design" from a panel of industry-leading investors, recognition for translating user needs into something they actually wanted to use.

CASE 03 / 04

A consumer site about sustainable fashion

Web · Frontend · IA Kode with Klossy · 2023 Designer + Dev

I led ideation, wireframing, and full-cycle development of "My Sustainable Guide", a consumer-facing site educating people about ethical fashion choices. Built end-to-end in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Received 90% approval from scholars, instructors, and Karlie Kloss.

CASE 04 / 04

A consumer brand campaign, shipped live

Brand · Strategy · Analytics Marimix × USC · 2025 to Present Strategy Lead

As Strategy & Analytics Lead on a live USC client engagement with Marimix (a consumer snack brand), I work alongside designers and brand leads to manage real ad spend across Reddit and brand-owned channels, translating campaign data into creative direction, audience targeting, and budget allocation across the funnel.

03, How I Work

A loose four-step rhythm, repeated until the work is right.

i.

Listen

Customer interviews, reviews, support tickets, raw data. The work starts here, not in Figma. Context is the moat.

ii.

Frame

Turn the noise into a question worth answering. What is the actual job to be done? What does success look like?

iii.

Ship

Wireframe, prototype, build, deploy. Move fast, but not at the expense of details. Everything should feel considered.

iv.

Learn

Measure what matters. Talk to people. Iterate. The first version is rarely the right one, that's why we ship.
