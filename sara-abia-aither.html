I'm a designer who came up through data.

After a BSc in Data Science from Warwick, I'm pursuing an MSc in Digital Social Media at USC, chasing the part of design that lives where research, story, and product converge. I think the best design comes from understanding people deeply, then sweating the details that make work feel inevitable.

I co-founded my family's boutique hotel during the pandemic and ran every part of the guest experience: website, brand, booking flow, in-room collateral, the whole system. I won "Best Design" at Goldman Sachs for a UX challenge, shipped a consumer site through Kode with Klossy, and now lead Analytics & Insights on a live consumer-brand campaign at USC.

I'm trilingual (English, French, Arabic), an active builder with AI tooling, and genuinely happiest when I'm jumping between a Figma file and a customer interview.
